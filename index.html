About Us

Radhey Nrutalayala Dance Classes was founded with the mission to preserve and promote the rich heritage of classical Indian dance forms. Our institution is dedicated to fostering artistic excellence and cultural appreciation through comprehensive dance education.

Our founder, Vaishnavi Bhat, is a renowned dancer and choreographer with over 10 years of experience in classical dance. Under his guidance, our team of highly skilled instructors brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to their teaching.

At Radhey Nrutalayala, we believe in nurturing the potential of each student through personalized instruction and a supportive learning environment. Our curriculum is designed to provide a deep understanding of dance techniques, history, and expressions, ensuring a well-rounded education.

Our Classes

Beginner Classes

Our beginner classes introduce students to the basics of Bharatanatyam, including foundational postures, steps, and simple dance sequences. Perfect for those new to classical dance or looking to refresh their basic skills.

Intermediate Classes

Intermediate classes build upon the basics and focus on more complex dance routines, intricate footwork, and expressive storytelling through dance. Designed for students with some prior experience in Bharatanatyam.

Advanced Classes

Advanced classes are tailored for experienced dancers looking to refine their technique, master advanced dance sequences, and perform on stage. These classes also cover the theoretical aspects of Bharatanatyam.

Special Workshops

We regularly host special workshops featuring guest instructors and artists. These workshops provide unique opportunities to learn new styles, techniques, and performance skills.
